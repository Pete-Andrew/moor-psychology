EMDR trauma groups (GTEP) for Adults and Teams

Have you or your team experienced a traumatic event? GTEP is an evidence-based group using EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitisation and reprocessing) to help reduce difficult feelings, learn ways of calming and soothing to help you feel more empowered in your life. Groups can be effective for all kinds of experiences including bullying, accidents and injuries, witnessing a trauma, difficult incidents in the work place, bereavements etc. The group is a closed group, so no one can join once the group has started to help everyone feel safe. In the group you will not be asked to talk about your trauma. Small groups can be run for adults and can be delivered as a 1 to 1, if preferable. Groups can help you feel less alone and are a cost effective way of having EMDR therapy and can give you a taster whether EMDR therapy may work for you.

If you are interested in joining a group please get in touch and we can offer you a free 15-minute consultation to discuss your suitability and provide more information.
