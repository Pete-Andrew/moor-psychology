EMDR Therapy for Children

EMDR is an evidence-based therapy. For more information, please follow this link to the EMDR UK Association website which explains more about the EMDR for children and young people. It may also be helpful to watch this video created for children which simply explains the therapy. On this page you can also find some helpful leaflets on understanding child trauma, a guide for parents and carers of young children and Understanding trauma, a guide for adolescents and parents. The EMDR UK website also has some helpful leaflets written as a story to explain EMDR therapy to young children and a useful leaflet on explaining how EMDR therapy works with children and young people.

EMDR groups can be delivered to children in creative and playful ways, using movement and art to make the group fun while supporting children to feel less upset about their difficult experiences. For more information on groups follow this link.
