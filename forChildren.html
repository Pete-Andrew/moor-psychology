For Children

We know that growing up brings many changes. At times it can be both exciting and scary. There is so much to discover, just like the precious shells you find on the beach. At times, things that happen to us may bring up big feelings. These can get in the way of us doing things we used to enjoy with family and friends.

All families and children are different and we hope to get to know more about you and your family, and what matters most to you all.

Meeting new people can feel scary, so we have lots of fun ways of helping to learn more about your life, like using art, making stories and playing games together. We have helped lots of children to understand big feelings so they don’t get in the way of having fun.

We offer bespoke integrative psychotherapy to children drawing on models including relational attachment, family therapy, cognitive and behavioural therapy and specialise in EMDR therapy for children.
