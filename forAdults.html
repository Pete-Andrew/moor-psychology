EMDR

At Ebb and Flow we specialise in offering EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitisation and Representing) therapy to adults who have experienced trauma in their childhoods or though accidents or injuries. As a certified EMDR practitioner we have specialist knowledge in supporting adults to process trauma through making sense of what has happened to you and help you feel calmer and more empowered in your life.
