Child EMDR Groups

EMDR groups can be delivered to children and young people creatively using movement and art to help engage children and young people sensitively in age-appropriate ways. Groups support children and young people to feel less distressed about their difficult experiences and help them develop ways to manage emotions as they arise. Groups can be useful if a few children oppressed young people have experienced a difficult event, for example, a sudden or traumatic bereavement of a friend or been involved in an accident. Groups are run over 1 or 2 sessions and can be offered to families and individuals. If you are interested in finding out more, please get in touch and we can offer you a free 15-minute consultation to provide more information and think about whether a group may be helpful.
