Frequent Asked Questions for Adults

How do I know what therapy is right for me?

Some people come to therapy clear about what therapy they are looking for others are looking for guidance. We will use our assessment (link to assessment section from specialist therapies section) to guide you about what therapy we think is best for the difficulty you are seeking support with.
How many therapy sessions will I need?

It is difficult to say how much therapy you may need as it will depend on what you are seeking support for. We can think about this with you in the assessment session and plan your therapy around your goals and budget.
How do I pay for appointments?

Appointment are invoiced when they booked and payment is due 48 hours prior to the appointment by direct bank transfer. Without payment the appointment will be cancelled. You are welcome to rebook.
What are the costs of therapy?

Cost vary depending on the service, so please get in touch and we can advise you based on your needs.
Do you work with insurance and private health care companies?

At Ebb and Flow we are registered with a number of insurance companies so please get in touch if you have private health care cover.

What if I need to cancel my appointment?

Appointments can be cancelled once booked but unfortunately if you give less than 24 hours notice the appointment will need to be paid for in full. This is because we cannot offer this to another to someone else.
