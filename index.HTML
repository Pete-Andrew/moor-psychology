Welcome to Ebb and Flow, we specialise in working with trauma, loss and bereavement offering a bespoke psychological support for children, parents, families and professionals based in Devon. We offer a range of individual, family and group therapies including EMDR (eye movement desensitisation and reprocessing therapy) and provide supervision and training to professionals.
