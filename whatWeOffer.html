For Children

We know that growing up brings many changes. At times it can be both exciting and scary. There is so much to discover, just like the precious shells you find on the beach. At times, things that happen to us may bring up big feelings. These can get in the way of us doing things we used to enjoy with family and friends. All families and children are different and we hope to get to know more about you and your family, and what matters most to you all. Meeting new people can feel scary, so we have lots of fun ways of helping to learn more about your life, like using art, making stories and playing games together. We have helped lots of children to understand big feelings so they don’t get in the way of having fun. We offer bespoke integrative therapy to children drawing on models including relational attachment, family therapy, cognitive and behavioural therapy and specialise in EMDR therapy for children.

For Young People

At Ebb and flow we offer young people and their families a place to explore their feelings and experiences. We recognise that growing up can feel difficult at times as young people face new experiences and develop their identity in a complex and changing world. At other times young people may have experiences that impact on their emotional well-being. This can feel confusing and over whelming and may be difficult to share with families and friends. Young people can feel alone and struggle with everyday life, such as school and relationships with friends and family. We offer bespoke integrative therapy to young people drawing on models including relational attachment, family therapy, cognitive and behavioural therapy and specialise in EMDR therapy.

Parents

As parents we know that bringing up our children can be joyful and challenging as we support them to grow and develop. As parents we can worry about how to get it ‘right’ for our children. It may be hard to reach out for support for lots of reasons including fearing judgement of others or having had experiences of not feeling heard when we have raised our worries. Being a parent presents a rewarding, challenging and complex journey. This can trigger a range of feelings in our bodies and may often remind us of of our own childhood experiences. It can be all too easy to feel stuck and on your own. At Ebb and Flow we offer a safe place to explore your unique experiences, process any difficult experiences and help you feel more empowered and confident in being a “good enough” parent. We also offer new parents' opportunities to explore their birth experiences and join with other new parents and their babies in creative ways. This will include making early memories, building your bond with your baby and learning ways of caring for yourself.

Families

We recognise that family life is about more than each of you as individuals. Family relationships often change relationships often change while children are growing up and when unexpected events happen., such as bereavements, young people leaving home and demanding life events. We can get stuck in familiar patterns of relating to each other which can make it harder to solve life problems. At Ebb and Flow we can offer specialist family therapy to support you in changing together.

Adults

At Ebb and Flow we specialise in offering EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitisation and Representing) therapy to adults who have experienced trauma in their childhoods or though accidents or injuries. As a certified EMDR practitioner we have specialist knowledge in supporting adults to process trauma through making sense of what has happened to you and help you feel calmer and more empowered in your life.
