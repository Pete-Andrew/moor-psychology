For Professionals

As experienced Psychologists we offer training, supervision, consultation to professionals and EMDR trauma groups for Teams.
Supervision At present we have 2 supervisor in our team and offer supervision to a range of multi disciplinary colleagues and Psychologists. Within this we are happy to offer mentoring to psychologists starting in private practice.
Meet our supervisors
Dr Lisa Clive, Clinical Psychologist.

I am an experienced supervisor with many years of experience of supervising colleagues within the NHS, higher education, the charity sector and private practice, I believe in the importance of offering safe and compassionate spaces in which the supervisees can be supported to explore their work and the impact this has on them. I am open to learning and see supervision as a space for professional and personal growth. I feel privileged to walk alongside others in their professional journeys. I specialise in childhood trauma, loss, bereavement and EMDR therapy with children and adults. .
