For Parents

As parents we know that bringing up our children can be joyful and challenging as we support them to grow and develop. As parents we can worry about how to get it ‘right’ for our children. It may be hard to reach out for support for lots of reasons including fearing judgement of others or having had experiences of not feeling heard when we have raised our worries.

Being a parent presents a rewarding, challenging and complex journey. This can trigger a range of feelings in our bodies and may often remind us of of our own childhood experiences. It can be all too easy to feel stuck and on your own.

At Ebb and Flow we offer a safe place to explore your unique experiences, process any difficult experiences and help you feel more empowered and confident in being a “good enough” parent.

To support you we offer specialist parenting therapy, parenting groups, family therapy and EMDR therapy. We have specialist training in family therapy, dyadic developmental psychotherapy, EMDR, babycalm© and toddlercalm©.
