For Young People

At Ebb and flow we offer young people and their families a place to explore their feelings and experiences. We recognise that growing up can feel difficult at times as young people face new experiences and develop their identity in a complex and changing world. At other times young people may have experiences that impact on their emotional well-being. This can feel confusing and over whelming and may be difficult to share with families and friends. Young people can feel alone and struggle with everyday life, such as school and relationships with friends and family.

We offer bespoke integrative therapy to young people drawing on models including relational attachment, family therapy, cognitive and behavioural therapy and specialise in EMDR therapy.
